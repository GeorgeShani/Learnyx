Our Culture at Learnyx

Discover what makes Learnyx a great place to work and grow your career.

Remote-First Environment

We believe great work happens when people have flexibility. Our team is distributed globally, with async communication as our default and regular virtual team building activities.

Continuous Learning

Every team member has an annual learning budget of $2,000 to spend on courses, conferences, books, or any learning resources. We also have monthly "Learn & Share" sessions where team members teach each other new skills.

Impact-Driven Work

Your work directly impacts millions of learners worldwide. We share monthly impact reports showing how our platform is helping people achieve their educational goals.

Collaborative Innovation

We encourage experimentation and creative problem-solving. Every team member can propose new ideas, and we dedicate 20% of our time to innovative projects and improvements.
